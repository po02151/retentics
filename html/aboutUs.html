About us

Enabling companies to leverage
AI tech to optimize business processes

We endeavor to solve the problems that many
businesses are facing by using AI-based data
analysis methodology.

We’ve started with the question: “Everybody knows data-driven decision is key to success but why can only few do it?”

Even though analyzing the data based on artificial intelligence becomes a must-have ability for successful companies, most companies are unable to establish the system due to lack of resources and experience, except for the big tech companies. Fridays lab pursues the goal of popularizing AI-based data analysis to make many different businesses establish data-driven decision-making systems.
